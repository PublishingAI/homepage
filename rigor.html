Case Studies - rigor and integrity

Integrity and originality

AI-powered services have been used to detect retraction, plagiarism, statistical inconsistency and error, and evaluate reference reliability, research reproducibility. These have largely contributed to the rigor of academic publishing.


Cases of use

  • 1. Statistics check - Stacheck's use in academic research evaluation, journals' quality check, and authors' workflow.

  • 2. Reference check - Scite are used to show citation contexts in evaluating journal's quality and reviewing manuscripts.

  • 3. Method Review - SciScore is used in publishing for assessing research reproducibility.

  • 4. Detect fake paper - Springer and Université Joseph Fourier release SciDetect to discover fake scientific papers.

  • 5. Against Plagiarism - Microbiology Society’s new open research platform, Access Microbiology, uses iThenticate to submitted papers for generating similarity scores.

Rigor criteria

AI-based technical checks targets at critical elements of an academic manuscript, such as the methodology, data availability statements, ethical statements, and examines the manuscripts' compliance with journal-specific editorial guidelines and rigor criteria. Publishers adopt them to support editorial decision-makings in the process of initial triage and preprint screening; it could also be included in the author workflow.


Cases of use

  • 1. Technical checks - Trinka AI helps authors who need to check their documents before submitting.

  • 2. Academic rigor - Ripeta's use for manuscript enhancement and preprints screening.

  • 3. Method Review - Methods Review Tool SciScore helps to promote reproducibility of research.

  • 4. Academic structures - BMJ Open used Penelope.ai to help authors verify academic structures of their manuscripts.

  • 5. Journal requirements - Microbiology Society’s Access Microbiology uses Penelope and SciScorto to assess the article according to the platform’s editorial and ethical requirements.

  • 6. Peer review - Artificial Intelligence Review Assistant allows for scale while at the same time upholding rigorous quality standards.
