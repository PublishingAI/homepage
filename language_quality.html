AI for improving language quality

Artificial-intelligence tools are trained with data to identify linguistic mistakes in terms of the grammar, spelling, punctuation, diction, and automatically suggest corrections and improvements to better suit academic writing styles in fractions of a second.

Authors can use these tools to proofread their manuscripts before submitting them to journals with better confidence in the language quality of their work. Editors can use them during initial triage, peer review and post-acceptence copyediting to support their editorial descison-makings.
