Case studies - TrendMD

TrendMD is an article discovery platform that generates related articles on journals, blogs, and other sites that academics, doctors, and researchers use in their day-to-day work. Scholarly publishers, authors, and funders use TrendMD to increase site traffic, reach their target audiences, and drive article impact. TrendMD uses AI technology to generate recommendations through a combination of semantic enrichment, collaborative filtering, and personalization.

An important feature of TrendMD is that recommendations that are shown in the widget are both from a publisher’s own content as well as from an equal number of recommendations from external sites. Publishers are happy with it because TrendMD has introduced to the industry - the TrendMD credit system that gives publishers traffic back in return for those who click on external links. This ability to earn credits means that publishers can get new readers just by installing the widget, at no cost. All credits within the system have a monetary value; they can unlock additional features that publishers can use to shape their traffic and accomplish specific business goals.
